About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer who loves to talk about design thinking. I believe great design happens when empathy meets strategy, and I'm passionate about creating solutions for people whose stories aren't being told properly.

My background is in psychology and neuroscience, particularly behavioral research. This foundation gives me a deep understanding of human wants and behavioral patterns, along with strong research skills that I bring to user experience design. I'm driven by the desire to participate in creating solutions that enrich people's lives.

I want to be surrounded by other design-thinkers where I can share ideas, learn, and have deep, mutually understanding discussions about the why and the how of design. I'm particularly passionate about accessibility thinking and creating inclusive products for as many users as possible.
